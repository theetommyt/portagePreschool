About

Preschool Info

Portage Preschool was founded to be a caring and safe environment, well equipped to prepare children to seamlessly integrate into the Portage school system. With over 60 years of experience between our staff we care for children in a holistic way, focusing not only on their learning environment but on their emotional growth and development as well.

For over 17 years parents have entrusted Judy Taylor and her teachers with providing their children with curriculum based lesson plans developed in collaboration with the school system that help develop preschoolers into kindergarten ready children.

A Great Start

Anecdotal evidence provided by the Portage Township School Corporation indicates that graduates of our preschool programs often perform at higher levels than other students. Many parents and teachers within the Portage Township Schools have commented on the success of our efforts to prepare students for kindergarten and beyond.
